# Maps & Leveling Spots

This page lists recommended **classic** leveling areas.

## Early Game (1–30)

- Training Grounds, beginner fields around towns
- Payon Field, Geffen Field, novice dungeons

## Mid Game (30–70)

- Payon Cave
- Orc Dungeon
- Byalan Dungeon
- Pyramids

## Late Game (70–99)

- High-level fields & dungeons appropriate to Episode 5
- Party leveling is heavily encouraged for efficiency

For a detailed monster list, see in-game databases or community guides.
