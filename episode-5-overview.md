# Episode 5 Overview

YmirRO starts at **Episode 5** of Ragnarok Online.

## What Episode 5 Includes

- Classic towns: Prontera, Geffen, Morroc, Payon, Alberta, Izlude and more.
- Early dungeons and fields with original spawn tables.
- Only gear, monsters, and cards that existed up to Episode 5.

## Progressive Episode Release

YmirRO is planned with **progressive episode releases**:
- Start at **Episode 5**
- Future episodes will be unlocked gradually
- No future content will skip ahead or add power creep suddenly

New content will always respect the **classic, fair** progression.
