# Rules & Server Philosophy

## Core Rules

1. No botting, macroing, or automation.
2. No RMT (real money trading).
3. No harassment, hate speech, or griefing.
4. Respect GMs and other players.
5. No exploiting bugs; report them instead.

## Philosophy

- **No Donation, No Custom, No GM Bias**
- **Pure Classic Episode 5**
- **Fair, level playing field**

Breaking rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
