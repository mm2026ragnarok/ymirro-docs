# Rules & Server Philosophy

YMIR Ragnarok Online is a classic Episode 5 server designed to preserve fairness, balance, and the original Ragnarok Online experience. All players are expected to follow these rules to maintain a healthy and competitive environment.

Failure to comply with these rules may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans depending on severity.

### Core Rules

#### 1. Automation & Cheating

The use of bots, macros, scripts, automation tools, hacks, packet editors, or modified clients is strictly prohibited.\
Attempting to bypass Gepard Shield or other security systems is not allowed.

Only the official YMIR Ragnarok Online client may be used to connect to the server.

***

#### 2. Bugs & Exploits

Exploiting bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics is prohibited.\
This includes map bugs, skill abuse, item duplication, NPC exploits, or any method that provides an unfair advantage.

All bugs must be reported to the staff immediately.\
Knowingly abusing bugs may result in rollbacks or permanent account penalties.

***

#### 3. Player Conduct

Players must treat others with respect at all times.

Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, threats, stalking, griefing, or intentional disruption of gameplay is not allowed.\
Healthy competition is encouraged, but toxic behavior will not be tolerated.

***

#### 4. Staff Interaction

Players must respect Game Masters and staff members.

Impersonating staff, spreading false information, or publicly accusing staff of bias without evidence is not permitted.\
Concerns, reports, and appeals must be handled through proper private channels.

***

#### 5. Economy, Trading & RMT

Real-money trading (RMT) is **allowed** on YMIR Ragnarok Online.

However, the following conditions apply:

* All RMT transactions are performed **at the player‚Äôs own risk**
* YMIR RO staff does not act as a middleman or provide transaction protection
* The server is not responsible for scams, chargebacks, or disputes related to RMT
* Fraud, impersonation, or scam attempts may still be penalized

Players are strongly advised to trade carefully and verify all transactions.

***

#### 6. Account Responsibility

Players are responsible for maintaining the security of their accounts, including passwords and login information.

YMIR Ragnarok Online is not responsible for:

* Accounts compromised due to weak passwords
* Losses caused by scams or player trades
* Consequences resulting from account sharing

All actions performed on an account are the responsibility of the account owner.

***

#### 7. Multi-Client & Gameplay Abuse

Dual client usage is permitted unless otherwise announced.

Using multiple accounts to abuse mechanics such as AFK farming, MVP monopolization, map control, or economic manipulation may result in staff intervention or penalties.

Gameplay should remain fair and reasonable for all players.
