# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is this server pay-to-win?**  
A: No. There are **no donations, no cash shop, and no custom items**.

**Q: What are the rates?**  
A: Base **5x**, Job **5x**, Drops **1x**.

**Q: Are there trans classes?**  
A: No. YmirRO is **pre-trans only** (99/50 max).

**Q: Can I dual client?**  
A: Yes, dual client is allowed within fair use.

**Q: Is Gepard Shield used?**  
A: Yes, the client is protected by **Gepard 3.0**.
